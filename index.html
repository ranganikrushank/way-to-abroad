Frequently Asked Questions

Clear, honest answers for Indian students & parents.

Is MBBS abroad valid in India? ▼
Yes, if the university is listed in the World Directory of Medical Schools (WDOMS) and recognized by the National Medical Commission (NMC) India. All our partner universities comply with these norms. Students must clear FMGE/NExT to practice in India.
What is the minimum NEET score required? ▼
As per NMC guidelines, you must qualify NEET-UG to be eligible for MBBS abroad. The cutoff varies yearly (usually 117+ for General category). We guide you on exact requirements per country.
Is the medium of instruction in English? ▼
Yes. All our recommended universities offer a dedicated English-medium track for international students. Local language classes are provided for clinical rotations and daily communication.
Do you charge hidden fees or commission? ▼
Absolutely not. We operate on a transparent counseling fee model. All university tuition, hostel, and government charges are paid directly to official portals. We provide itemized invoices for every payment.
How is safety and accommodation handled? ▼
Universities provide secure, Indian-friendly hostels with Indian food options. We assign a local coordinator, share emergency contacts, and conduct monthly check-ins. CCTV security, biometric access, and 24/7 campus patrols are standard.
